Healthcare demo notes

Use patient_id as the entity key, admission_date and discharge_date for utilization, cohort for treatment or control comparison, outcome_score as the outcome metric, duration_days as the survival duration proxy, and event as the event indicator.
Repeated admissions for the same patient within 30 days should be interpreted as readmission candidates.
Outcome_score is a simplified outcome field for comparison only and should not be treated as a clinical recommendation.
