Decision Intelligence Agent

A darker, staged workspace for a business stakeholder-led review: define the question, send the data to the analyst, approve the engineering layer, and inspect the final visuals in a dedicated visualization studio.

How to use it

Start in the intake workspace, send the data to the analyst, review the grounded findings, then approve the engineering step to materialize the bronze, silver, and gold layers plus the final visuals.

Interaction model

Each desk becomes actionable only after the previous stage finishes. Click the report buttons on the desks to switch the active workspace below without losing the overall flow.
